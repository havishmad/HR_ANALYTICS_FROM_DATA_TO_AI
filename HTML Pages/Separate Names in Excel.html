Excel Formula Methods for Name Extraction

Different approaches to extract first and last names in Excel
Method Formula for First Name Formula for Last Name Compatibility
1. LEFT & RIGHT with FIND =LEFT(A2, FIND(" ", A2)-1) LEFT & RIGHT with FIND Works in all Excel versions
2. TEXTBEFORE & TEXTAFTER =TEXTBEFORE(A2," ") LEFT & RIGHT with FIND Excel 365 & Excel 2021
3. MID with FIND & LEN =LEFT(A2, FIND(" ", A2)-1) LEFT & RIGHT with FIND Works in all Excel versions
4. FILTERXML & SUBSTITUTE =FILTERXML("<t><s>" & SUBSTITUTE(A2," ","</s><s>") & "</s></t>","//s[1]") =FILTERXML("<t><s>" & SUBSTITUTE(A2," ","</s><s>") & "</s></t>","//s[2]") Works in Excel 2013+ (with XML functions)
5. Flash Fill (No Formula) Manually type First Name → Press Ctrl + E Manually type Last Name → Press Ctrl + E Works in Excel 2013+
